A Simple Explanation of Grade 10 Physics, by Ryan Biju Sebastian

5 on 5 stars.
This is a book written by a young boy, Ryan, who aims to explain significant and rather complex phenomena in simplistic language. The book has got a good number of downloads, and no bad review so far. It's a good book which is apt for those who want to understand the concept over simply memorize the definitions. Best of all, sticks precisely to the Grade 10 syllabus.
